Echeveria Jade Point

About Echeveria Jade Point

Echeveria 'Jade Point' is a stunning succulent that captivates with its compact, rosette-forming foliage and elegant charm. Its vibrant green leaves, often tinged with soft pink or red edges when exposed to sunlight, exude a natural sophistication that makes it a favorite among succulent enthusiasts. The leaves are smooth and fleshy, spiraling gracefully to create a symmetrical rosette that can grow up to 6 inches in diameter.
